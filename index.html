All About Ducks

Their Origin

The origin of ducks is shrouded in mystery. Some believe it was at the creation of the earth. Some believe they simply fell from the sky one day. But it is whispered that they are the result of a bald eagle being struck by lightning.

Cool things about ducks:

  • Cool Hair
  • Big webbed feet
  • Big bills
  • Cute little ducklings
